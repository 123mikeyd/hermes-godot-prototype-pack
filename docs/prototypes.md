# Prototype Notes

## Crystal Dash

Crystal Dash tests the fastest possible Godot loop: single scene, procedural drawing, timer, collectibles, win/loss feedback.

Future upgrades:

- ghost replay route
- combo multiplier for clean paths
- moving crystals
- level seed selector

## Meteor Umbrella

Meteor Umbrella tests survival pressure and readable hazard pacing.

Future upgrades:

- shield cooldown
- wind lanes
- meteor warning shadows
- online daily seed

## Orbit Shooter

Orbit Shooter tests simple projectile/enemy systems and arcade scoring.

Future upgrades:

- enemy types
- powerups
- boss wave
- screenshake and particles
